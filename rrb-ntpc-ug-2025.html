Pay Scale & Working Culture

NTPC undergraduate posts 7th CPC ke Pay Level-2 & Level-3 me aate hain. Example posts: Junior Clerk-cum-Typist, Accounts Clerk, Train Clerk, Commercial-cum-Ticket Clerk, etc.

Basic Pay – Level-2 (approx.)
  • Basic Pay: लगभग ₹19,900/- per month
  • DA + HRA + TA + अन्य allowances extra
  • Approx In-hand (city ke hisaab se): ₹30,000+ per month
Basic Pay – Level-3 (approx.)
  • Basic Pay: लगभग ₹21,700/- per month
  • DA, HRA, Night Duty Allowance, आदि included
  • Approx In-hand: ₹35,000+ per month (zone / posting par depend)

Exact post-wise pay matrix aur allowances ke liye official notification jarur dekhein.

Age Limit (as on 01-01-2025)

  • Minimum Age: 18 Years
  • Maximum Age: 33 Years
  • Upper age relaxation as per Railway rules (SC / ST / OBC / PwD / ESM).

Eligibility (12th Pass)

  • 10+2 / Intermediate exam from any recognised Board in India.
  • General / OBC / EWS: minimum 50% marks (post wise conditions dekhें).
  • Typing requirement for Clerk / Typist posts:
    • English Typing: 30 WPM
    • Hindi Typing: 25 WPM
  • Medical standard & physical requirement RRB rules ke अनुसार.

Selection Process

  • CBT-1 (Common Computer Based Test)
  • CBT-2 (Post wise)
  • Typing Test / Skill Test (agar post me required ho)
  • Document Verification & Medical Examination
  • Final merit list CBT marks + qualifying tests ke basis par.

  • Step-5: Photo, signature & documents upload karein (notification ke format me).
  • Step-6: Application fee online pay karein aur final submit karein.
  • Step-7: Final submitted form ka print / PDF save kar lena future use ke liye.
